Really Fast & Really Angry

Instructions

You begin with a starting number (MPH). Each car is worth a differnet amount of points. The points are hidden until you click on the car. Clicking on a car will increase your "Speed". You win the game by matching your "Speed" to the starting number (MPH). You lose the game by going over the starting number (MPH), causing your engine to explode. Each new game randomizes the value of each car.
